Privacy Policy

Updated Date: 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LooksPro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data and facial image information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your data when you use the App.

2. What Data We Collect & Why

Face/Photo Data: When you upload or take a photograph for analysis, we use that image solely to provide you with aesthetic feedback and personalized recommendations.

Basic Personal Information: We may collect minimal data such as gender for accurate AI-based results.

Usage Data: We collect information on how you interact with the App (features used, session length, errors) to improve performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

• To provide the main service: analyzing images and offering recommendations.

• To improve and enhance our algorithms and App functionality.

• For security, fraud prevention, and to comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your uploaded photos and analysis data are retained only as long as needed for the service (for 15 days) and then automatically deleted. We do not keep your face images indefinitely or use them for other purposes.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your face data or personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising. We may disclose data only when required by law or legal process.

6. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures (such as encryption in transit and secure storage) to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no system is perfectly secure, so please understand a residual risk remains.

7. Your Rights & Controls

• You can request to access, correct or delete your data at any time by contacting us or using the in-App settings.

• You can withdraw your consent or discontinue use of the App whenever you like.

• If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the App without consent, please contact us and we’ll take prompt action.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 10 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you become aware of a child under this age using the App, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy with the updated “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

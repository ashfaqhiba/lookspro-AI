Privacy Policy for LooksPro

Last Updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

LooksPro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. We collect photographs and selfies that you upload to the application, voice recordings that you create within the application, and usage data through Firebase Analytics. Payment information is processed securely through RevenueCat and we do not store payment details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your photographs and voice recordings are used solely for the purpose of providing facial analysis, beauty recommendations, and voice coaching feedback. These media files are permanently deleted immediately after processing is complete. We use Firebase to send push notifications and analyze app usage patterns to improve your experience.

3. Data Storage & Deletion

We implement appropriate technical measures to ensure the secure processing of your data. All photographs and voice recordings are permanently deleted immediately after processing is completed. Users may request deletion of their associated data by contacting us directly at our support email.

4. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for subscription management and Firebase for push notifications and analytics. We do not share your personal data with any third-party AI providers or analysis services beyond these necessary service providers.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for users under 10 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age threshold.
